Welcome to Ethical Freelancing

An evolving ecosystem where clients work directly with freelancers, fostering higher ethics.

How this works:

Freelancers:

  • Host their own websites
  • Own their own identities
  • Build and own their reputations
  • Can’t be deplatformed

Clients:

  • Hire freelancers directly
  • Get better search results (not paid-for responses)
  • Get only as many proposals as requested, and only from best matches
  • Communicate via email or chosen means

Both Clients and Freelancers:

  • Pay no platform fees
  • Agree on fair terms

Clients can help create the Ethical Freelancing Ecosystem by:

  • Start hiring freelancers directly using registry below (small now but will grow quickly)
  • Join the volunteer development team at Slack or email us at ethicalfreelancing1@gmail.com.
